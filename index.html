Platform overview

Three pillars of medical aesthetics technology.

01

Device marketplace

A curated marketplace of FDA-cleared and CE-marked medical aesthetics devices from leading manufacturers including Cutera, Sciton, InMode, Syneron-Candela, and Lumenis, with verified dealer listings, certified pre-owned inventory, lease and finance options, and transparent pricing that removes the opacity from medical device procurement. Clinics and medspas can compare devices by modality, treatment indication, clinical evidence, and total cost of ownership in a single platform rather than navigating fragmented manufacturer sales channels and trade show acquisition processes.

02

Clinical training platform

Certified training programmes for medical aesthetics practitioners covering device operation, treatment protocols, patient selection criteria, adverse event management, and outcome optimisation across the full range of energy-based and injectables-adjacent device modalities. Device-specific certification programmes linked to manufacturer training requirements reduce clinical risk and ensure practitioners can deliver consistent, evidence-based outcomes that justify premium treatment pricing and support high patient retention rates in competitive aesthetics markets.

03

Practice performance tools

Business intelligence and practice management tools designed specifically for medical aesthetics clinics and medspas, covering treatment room utilisation analytics, device return-on-investment tracking, patient lifetime value modelling, and marketing attribution for device-specific treatment packages. Revenue optimisation tools identify underperforming treatment modalities, benchmark clinic performance against comparable practices, and model the financial impact of adding or retiring devices from the treatment menu based on actual clinic data.

Medical device companies and distributors in the energy-based aesthetics segment who want a consumer-accessible brand for a device portfolio or marketplace platform. Anti-aging and longevity brands
